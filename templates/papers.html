Derivations

Below are the various topics where I have derived and explored certain concepts. This started as a way for me to learn the LaTeX language, but then it became a small hobby.

Quantum Mechanics

The study of matter and energy at the atomic and subatomic scale. Covers wave-particle duality, uncertainty principle, quantum states, and fundamental particle interactions.

Optics

The branch of physics dealing with light behaviour and properties. Includes geometric optics, wave optics, and electromagnetic theory of light propagation.

Mechanics

The study of motion, forces, and energy in physical systems. Encompasses classical mechanics, fluid dynamics, and the principles of conservation.

Electromagnetism

Study of electric and magnetic fields and their interactions. Covers Maxwell's equations, electromagnetic waves, and charged particle behavior.

Relativity

Einstein's theories of special and general relativity. Explores spacetime, gravity as curved geometry, and the relationship between mass and energy.

Mathematics

Mathematical techniques and proofs essential for physics. Includes calculus, differential equations, linear algebra, and complex analysis applications.
